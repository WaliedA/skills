# Intuitio Ventures Fund Structure Reference

Reference file documenting the actual entity structure, governance terms, and document conventions used in Intuitio Ventures Fund I, LP. Use this when the user asks to draft, review, or modify documents consistent with the existing Intuitio Ventures structure, or when onboarding new venture partners or LPs.

## Entity Structure

The fund uses a standard three-entity Delaware structure administered by Decile Partners / VC Lab:

```
Intuitio Ventures Fund I, LP          (Delaware LP — the Fund)
  └── GP: Intuitio Ventures GP, LLC   (Delaware LLC — General Partner)
        └── Manager: Intuitio Ventures, LLC  (Delaware LLC — Management Company)
```

All three entities were formed on March 17, 2022 with the Delaware Secretary of State. Registered agent for all entities is The Incorporators Ltd., 300 Creek View Road, Suite 209, Newark, New Castle County, Delaware 19711.

## GP LLC — Stewardship Agreement

The GP LLC Operating Agreement is called the "Stewardship Agreement" (VC Lab template convention). Key structural features:

### Unit Classes
- **Class A Units:** 950,000 authorized. Voting (1 vote per unit). Issued to active investors/partners. 720,000 issued to initial Class A Members. Transfers require Manager approval; upon transfer, Class A Units automatically convert to Class V Units (unless Manager determines otherwise).
- **Share Pool:** 230,000 Class A Units reserved for issuance to venture partners and future partners. Manager can issue Share Pool units at sole discretion without consent of other Members.
- **Class V Units:** 50,000 initially authorized (held by VC Lab). Non-voting. Anti-dilution protection (cannot be diluted without unanimous Class V consent). Auto-increase upon conversion of Class A to Class V.

### Carry Economics
- **Carry Share:** GP's entitlement to carried interest from the Fund LP (and any other managed vehicles).
- **Preferred Share:** Paid first from Carry Share to Class V holders. Calculated as: (issued Class V Units / 1,000,000) × Carry Share. At 50,000 Class V Units, this equals 5% of Carry Share.
- **Class A residual:** Carry Share minus Preferred Share, distributed pro rata to Class A holders.
- **Distribution waterfall:** Class V holders receive Preferred Share first, then Class A holders receive remainder.

### Governance
- **Manager:** Intuitio Ventures, LLC has sole authority to manage GP. Can be removed by Majority of Interests (66⅔% of Class A Units).
- **Capital Commitment:** Manager responsible for GP's capital commitment to the Fund (1% of total LP commitments).
- **Partnership Representative:** VC Lab, Inc. (for tax purposes under IRC § 6223).

### Key Protections
- Class V exculpation: Class V holders have no liability; covenant not to sue by Manager and Class A holders.
- All indemnification and expense obligations allocated to Class A accounts only.
- Amendments affecting Class V require consent of (i) majority Class A, (ii) majority Class V, and (iii) VC Lab approval.

### Dispute Resolution
Mandatory mediation under AAA Commercial Mediation Procedures (San Francisco), then Delaware Chancery Court. Class V holders entitled to attorneys' fees.

## Management Company — Alignment Agreement

The Management Company LLC Agreement is called the "Alignment Agreement" (VC Lab template convention). Key features:

### Unit Classes
- **Class A Units:** Walied Elbashir holds 900,000 (100%). Consideration: $90.00.
- **Profits Units:** Available up to 10% of total outstanding Units. Issued under a Profits Interest Plan. Non-voting. Subject to Incentive Hurdle (profits interest treatment under Rev. Proc. 93-27).

### Manager Terms
- **Manager:** Walied Abdelrahman Mohamed Elbashir
- **Time Commitment:** Part Time, 40%
- **Vesting:** Units are NOT subject to vesting (per the agreement terms).

### Governance
- Majority of Interests: 66⅔% of outstanding Class A Units.
- Majority of Managers: 66⅔% of Class A Units held by Managers.
- Tie Breaker Vote: 66⅔% of LP capital commitments in the Fund.
- Major decisions requiring Majority of Interests: amendments, asset sales, mergers/dissolution.

### Termination / Departure Provisions
- Manager resignation/termination: 100% of Unvested Units forfeited; Company has option to purchase up to 100% of vested Units at Fair Market Value (9-month exercise window).
- Reduced time commitment: 50% of Unvested Units forfeited; Company can purchase up to 50% of vested Units.
- Change in Control: 100% acceleration of Unvested Units.
- Death: Units transfer to heirs with joinder; Company has 9-month purchase option at FMV.

### Distributions
- Pro rata by Units held.
- Profits Units subject to Incentive Hurdle (no distributions until aggregate post-issuance distributions exceed the hurdle).
- Tax distributions: quarterly estimated tax payments to Members.
- All distributions subject to clawback, reserves, and the Company's financial ability.

## Venture Partner Onboarding

When onboarding a new Venture Partner to the Intuitio Ventures structure, the mechanism is:

1. **GP Level (Stewardship Agreement):** Issue Class A Units from the 230,000 Share Pool. Manager has sole discretion over terms. No consent of other Members required for Share Pool issuance.

2. **Management Company Level (Alignment Agreement):** If the VP will participate in management fee economics, issue Profits Units under the Profits Interest Plan (capped at 10% of total Units). Requires written award agreement setting Incentive Hurdle.

3. **Separate VP Agreement:** For deal sourcing, fundraising, and service terms, execute a standalone Venture Partner Agreement referencing the GP and Fund structure. The VP Agreement governs the relationship; the unit grants in the Stewardship/Alignment Agreements govern the economics.

### VP Carry Allocation Path
Carry flows: Fund LP → GP LLC (Carry Share) → Class V Preferred Share (to VC Lab) → Remainder to Class A holders (including VPs who hold Share Pool units) pro rata.

A VP's carry participation is determined by the number of Class A Units issued from the Share Pool relative to total Class A Units outstanding.

### VP Management Fee Participation Path
Management fees flow: Fund LP → Management Company (Intuitio Ventures, LLC) → distributed to Members pro rata by Units. VP participates if they hold Profits Units (subject to Incentive Hurdle) or if a separate management fee sharing arrangement is documented in their VP Agreement.
