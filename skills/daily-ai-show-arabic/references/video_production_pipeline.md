# Video Production Pipeline — يومياتي مع الذكاء الاصطناعي

## Complete Production Guide: From Script to Published YouTube Video

This document covers three production paths, from fully AI-automated (fastest, lowest cost) to hybrid (best quality), to fully manual (highest production value). Pick the path that matches your current stage.

---

## PATH A: Fully AI-Automated (Recommended to Start)
**Time per episode:** 30-45 minutes | **Cost:** ~$50-100/month | **Quality:** 7/10

This path uses AI avatar + AI voice to produce the entire show without filming.

### Tools Required

| Step | Tool | Cost | Purpose |
|------|------|------|---------|
| Script | Claude (this skill) | Included in your plan | Generate Arabic script from email newsletters |
| AI Presenter | **HeyGen** (recommended) | $29-99/month | AI avatar reads your Arabic script with lip sync |
| Voice Clone | HeyGen Voice Clone | Included in Creator+ | Clone YOUR voice so the avatar sounds like you |
| Thumbnail | **Canva** | Free-$13/month | Create Arabic thumbnails from the concepts |
| Captions | HeyGen auto-captions | Included | Arabic + English subtitles |
| Upload | YouTube Studio | Free | Publish with SEO package |

### Step-by-Step Process

#### 1. Generate the Script (5 min)
Tell Claude: "جهز حلقة اليوم" or "prepare today's AI show"
The skill searches your Gmail, extracts stories, generates all 4 deliverables.

#### 2. Set Up HeyGen (one-time, 30 min)
- Create account at heygen.com
- **Voice Clone**: Record 30 seconds of yourself reading Arabic. HeyGen creates a voice clone that sounds like you in Arabic. This is the key differentiator: the avatar speaks in YOUR voice.
- **Choose Avatar**: Pick a professional male avatar from their 700+ library, or create a Digital Twin from your own photo (Creator plan+).
- **Create Template**: Set up a reusable news show template with:
  - Background: A clean tech/AI themed studio backdrop
  - Lower thirds: Show branding "يومياتي مع الذكاء الاصطناعي"
  - Consistent framing and lighting

#### 3. Produce the Video (15-20 min)
- Open HeyGen AI Studio
- Paste the Arabic script from `script_ar.md` into HeyGen
- Select your avatar and voice clone
- For each story segment, you can:
  - Add B-roll images (screenshots of the products/companies mentioned)
  - Add text overlays for key numbers and technical terms
  - Add transition slides between stories
- Click Generate. HeyGen renders the full video in 10-20 minutes.

#### 4. Add Captions (5 min)
- HeyGen auto-generates Arabic captions
- Review and fix any transliteration errors in technical terms
- Optionally add English subtitle track for wider reach

#### 5. Create Thumbnail (5 min)
- Open Canva, use the concepts from `thumbnails.md`
- Use a bold Arabic font (Cairo Black or Tajawal Bold)
- Size: 1280x720 pixels
- Export as PNG

#### 6. Upload to YouTube (5 min)
- Upload video to YouTube Studio
- Copy-paste title, description, and tags from `seo_package.md`
- Upload thumbnail
- Set language to Arabic
- Add to your show playlist
- Schedule or publish

## PATH B: Hybrid (Best Balance)
**Time per episode:** 1-2 hours | **Cost:** ~$100-150/month | **Quality:** 8.5/10

You record your voice (real, not cloned), and use AI for visuals and editing.

### Tools Required

| Step | Tool | Cost | Purpose |
|------|------|------|---------|
| Script | Claude (this skill) | Included | Generate Arabic script |
| Voice Recording | **ElevenLabs** or your phone | $5-22/month or free | Record or enhance your Arabic narration |
| Teleprompter | **BIGVU** or **PromptSmart** | $10-20/month or free | Read script naturally while recording |
| Video Assembly | **CapCut** (recommended) or **Descript** | Free or $24/month | Edit: add B-roll, text overlays, transitions |
| B-Roll Generation | **Grok Imagine** or **Runway** | $4.20/min or $15/month | Generate AI visuals for story illustrations |
| Thumbnail | Canva | Free-$13/month | Arabic thumbnails |
| Captions | CapCut auto-captions | Free | Arabic subtitles |

### Step-by-Step Process

#### 1. Generate Script (5 min)
Same as Path A.

#### 2. Record Voiceover (20-30 min)
**Option A: Record on your phone**
- Use BIGVU teleprompter app: paste the Arabic script, it scrolls as you read
- Record in a quiet room
- Speak naturally, following the script's Gulf+MSA tone
- You can record audio-only (no camera) if you prefer a voiceover-style show

**Option B: Use ElevenLabs**
- Paste the Arabic script into ElevenLabs
- Select an Arabic voice (Gulf accent available) or clone your own voice
- Generate high-quality narration
- Cost: ~$0.30 per 1,000 characters

#### 3. Assemble the Video (30-45 min)
In CapCut (free, powerful, great Arabic support):
- Import your audio/video recording
- Add story title cards between segments (Arabic text on branded backgrounds)
- Add B-roll: screenshots, company logos, product demos
- Add text overlays for key stats: "442 توكن/ثانية" over Nvidia segment
- Add transitions between stories (simple cuts or slides)
- Add background music (subtle, tech/news feel, royalty-free)
- Add Arabic captions (CapCut auto-generates them)

#### 4. Generate AI B-Roll (10 min)
For stories that need illustration:
- Use Grok Imagine ($4.20/min) or Runway for short clips showing concepts
- Use Google Image Search for company logos and product screenshots
- Use screen recordings of actual AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) as B-roll

#### 5. Export, Thumbnail, Upload (10 min)
- Export at 1080p from CapCut
- Create thumbnail in Canva
- Upload with SEO package to YouTube

## PATH C: Professional Studio (Highest Quality)
**Time per episode:** 2-4 hours | **Cost:** ~$200-500/month | **Quality:** 9.5/10

You film yourself presenting on camera with professional setup.

### Equipment

| Item | Recommendation | Cost (one-time) |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Camera | iPhone 15/16 or Sony ZV-1 | Already own / $400-800 |
| Microphone | Rode Wireless ME or Hollyland Lark M2 | $100-200 |
| Lighting | Elgato Key Light or ring light | $50-200 |
| Teleprompter | Parrot Teleprompter (iPad mount) | $100-200 |
| Backdrop | Green screen or clean wall + Elgato panels | $50-150 |
| Editing | DaVinci Resolve (free) or Final Cut Pro | Free / $300 |

### Setup (one-time, 2-3 hours)
- Mount camera at eye level
- Position lights: key light at 45 degrees, fill light opposite
- Set up teleprompter with iPad showing the Arabic script
- Design consistent intro/outro animation (hire on Fiverr for $50-100)
- Create lower-third templates in your editor

### Daily Process
1. Generate script with Claude skill (5 min)
2. Load script into teleprompter (2 min)
3. Film yourself presenting (20-30 min, with retakes)
4. Edit in DaVinci Resolve: cut mistakes, add B-roll, titles, captions (60-90 min)
5. Create thumbnail, upload with SEO package (10 min)

## Recommended Starting Path

**Start with Path A (fully automated) for the first 2 weeks.**
Why: You need to validate the concept, build a content rhythm, and test audience reception before investing in equipment or recording skills. HeyGen at $29/month lets you publish daily with minimal friction. If the audience responds, upgrade to Path B (hybrid) for authenticity, since your real voice will build stronger audience connection.

### 30-Day Launch Plan

| Week | Action | Path |
|------|--------|------|
| Week 1 | Set up HeyGen, voice clone, publish 5 episodes | Path A |
| Week 2 | Continue daily, analyze YouTube Analytics | Path A |
| Week 3 | Experiment with Path B (record your voice for 2 episodes) | Path B |
| Week 4 | Compare metrics, decide which path to commit to | A or B |

## Platform-Specific Tips

### YouTube Algorithm for Arabic Content
- Post consistently at the same time daily (morning UAE time: 7-8 AM is ideal)
- First 48 hours are critical for the algorithm
- Arabic content has less competition in AI/tech niche than English
- Use both Arabic and English tags for discoverability
- YouTube Shorts: Extract the strongest 60-second story from each episode as a Short
- Community posts: Share a poll about which story was most interesting

### Arabic-Specific Considerations
- Right-to-left text in thumbnails and overlays needs careful design
- Arabic auto-captions quality varies. Always review technical terms
- Gulf Arabic viewers prefer shorter, punchier segments (hence 1.5-2 min per story)
- Use Arabic numerals in text overlays but Western digits in parentheses for clarity

### Monetization Path
- YouTube Partner Program: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours
- Sponsorships: AI tool companies will want to reach your Arabic tech audience
- Paid newsletter/Telegram channel: Deeper analysis behind paywall
- Consulting: "The AI guy in Arabic" brand positions you for advisory work
