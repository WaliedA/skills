## Story De-duplication

Multiple newsletters often cover the same story. When you find the same news across 2+ sources:
- Use the most detailed source for facts
- Note that multiple sources covered it (increases story importance)
- Cite the primary source in the script, mention others as confirmation
- Example: "وهالخبر غطته أكثر من نشرة، من The Batch إلى Axios، وهذا يدل على أهميته"
