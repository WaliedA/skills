## Estonia

### Entity Formation
| Filing | Authority | Timing | Cost (approx.) |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| OÜ registration | Commercial Register (Äriregister) via e-Business Register | 1-3 business days (digital) | EUR 190 state fee |
| Articles of association filing | Commercial Register | Simultaneous with registration | Included |
| UBO (beneficial owner) registration | Commercial Register | At formation; update within 30 days of changes | Free |
| VAT registration | Estonian Tax and Customs Board | Within 3 days of exceeding EUR 40,000 turnover (or voluntary) | Free |

### Ongoing Compliance
| Obligation | Authority | Frequency | Deadline |
|------------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Annual report filing | Commercial Register | Annual | 6 months after financial year end (June 30 for calendar year) |
| Corporate income tax return | Tax and Customs Board | Monthly (if distributions made) | 10th of the month following distribution |
| VAT return | Tax and Customs Board | Monthly | 20th of the following month |
| Social tax / payroll reporting | Tax and Customs Board | Monthly | 10th of the following month |
| UBO update | Commercial Register | As needed | Within 30 days of change |
| Transfer pricing documentation | Tax and Customs Board | Annual (if thresholds met) | With annual report |

### Fund Manager / AIFMD (Estonian Finantsinspektsioon)

#### Sub-Threshold Registration (< EUR 100M leveraged / < EUR 500M unleveraged)
| Filing | Details | Timing |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Registration notification | Notify Finantsinspektsioon of: identity of manager, funds managed, investment strategies, main exposures, principal concentrations | Before commencing management activities |
| Annual reporting | Report on: principal instruments traded, principal exposures, most important concentrations | Annually |
| Threshold monitoring | If AUM approaches full-scope threshold, notify FI and apply for authorization | Ongoing |
| AML/KYC registration | Register as an obligated entity under Estonian AML Act | At registration |
| Risk assessment | Document fund-level AML risk assessment | At launch; update annually |

#### Full-Scope Authorization (above thresholds)
| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Application | Detailed application including: organizational structure, risk management procedures, valuation procedures, delegation arrangements, conflicts of interest policy |
| Depositary | Must appoint an independent depositary (EU credit institution or investment firm) |
| Capital requirement | EUR 125,000 base + 0.02% of AUM above EUR 250M (cap EUR 10M) |
| Reporting | Full AIFMD Annex IV reporting (quarterly for large managers) |
| Remuneration policy | AIFMD-compliant remuneration policy for identified staff |
| Passport | Can market to professional investors across EU via AIFMD passport |

### Data Protection (GDPR via Andmekaitse Inspektsioon)
| Obligation | Timing |
|------------|--------|
| Records of Processing Activities (ROPA) | Maintain continuously; available on request |
| Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) | Before high-risk processing activities |
| Data Protection Officer (DPO) appointment | Required if: public authority, large-scale systematic monitoring, or large-scale processing of special categories |
| Data breach notification | 72 hours to supervisory authority; "without undue delay" to affected individuals if high risk |
| Privacy notice to data subjects | Before or at time of data collection |

## Delaware (US)

### LP Formation
| Filing | Authority | Timing | Cost (approx.) |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Certificate of Limited Partnership | Delaware Secretary of State | 1-2 business days (standard); same-day (expedited) | $200 filing fee |
| Registered agent appointment | Private registered agent service | At formation | $50-300/year |
| EIN (tax ID) application | IRS | After formation | Free |
| Bank account opening | US bank | After EIN | Varies |

### Ongoing Compliance
| Obligation | Authority | Frequency | Deadline / Cost |
|------------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Delaware LP annual tax | Delaware Secretary of State | Annual | $300; due June 1 |
| Federal tax return (Form 1065) | IRS | Annual | March 15 (or extension to September 15) |
| K-1 distribution to LPs | IRS / LPs | Annual | With Form 1065 |
| State tax filings | Various state tax authorities | Annual | Varies by state where LPs reside |
| FATCA reporting | IRS | Annual | If foreign financial institution reporting applies |
| Beneficial ownership report (BOI) | FinCEN | At formation; update within 30 days of changes | Free |

### SEC and Securities Filings
| Filing | Authority | Timing | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Form D (Regulation D) | SEC | Within 15 days of first sale | Required if relying on Reg D exemption |
| Blue sky filings | State securities regulators | Varies by state | Required in states where LPs reside; some use NASAA electronic filing |
| Form ADV (if registered) | SEC | Annual amendment within 90 days of fiscal year end | Only if registered as investment adviser |
| Form PF (if applicable) | SEC | Quarterly or annually based on AUM | For registered advisers to private funds |

### Investment Advisers Act Exemptions
| Exemption | Requirements |
|-----------|-------------|
| Foreign Private Adviser (§ 202(a)(30)) | < 15 US clients/investors; < $25M AUM attributable to US persons; no US place of business; not an adviser to any registered investment company |
| Private Fund Adviser (§ 203(m)) | Only advises qualifying private funds; < $150M AUM in US; no SEC registration required but must file reports |
| Venture Capital Fund Adviser (§ 203(l)) | Only advises VC funds (as defined); no AUM limit; exempt from registration but must file as exempt reporting adviser |

## UAE

### Entity Formation (Mainland LLC)
| Filing | Authority | Timing | Cost (approx.) |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Trade name reservation | DED (Dept. of Economic Development) | 1-2 days | AED 620 |
| LLC registration | DED | 3-5 business days | AED 10,000-15,000+ |
| Trade license | DED | With registration | AED 10,000+ (varies by activity) |
| Office lease (Ejari registration) | RERA / Ejari | Required before license | Varies |
| Immigration card | GDRFA | After license | AED 3,000+ |
| VAT registration | Federal Tax Authority | Mandatory if turnover > AED 375,000 | Free |

### DIFC (Financial Free Zone) — If Managing Fund from Dubai
| Filing | Authority | Timing | Cost |
|--------|-----------|--------|------|
| DFSA (Dubai Financial Services Authority) license | DFSA | 3-6 months | $10,000+ application + annual fees |
| DIFC company registration | DIFC Registrar of Companies | After DFSA license | $8,000+ |
| Data Protection registration | DIFC Commissioner of Data Protection | At commencement | $500/year |

### Ongoing UAE Compliance
| Obligation | Frequency | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| CIT return | Annual | 9% on profits > AED 375,000 (mainland); 0% on qualifying free zone income |
| VAT return | Quarterly or monthly | Based on turnover |
| Trade license renewal | Annual | DED or free zone authority |
| UBO disclosure | At formation + updates | Required under AML regulations |
| ESR (Economic Substance Regulations) | Annual | Report demonstrating adequate substance in UAE |
| AML compliance | Ongoing | Appoint MLRO; maintain CDD records; file SARs |

## Ethiopia

### Entity Formation (PLC)
| Filing | Authority | Timing | Cost |
|--------|-----------|--------|------|
| Investment permit | Ethiopian Investment Commission (EIC) | 2-4 weeks | Varies |
| Company registration | Ministry of Trade and Regional Integration | After investment permit | ETB 5,000+ |
| Tax identification number (TIN) | Ethiopian Revenue and Customs Authority | After registration | Free |
| Business license | Regional trade bureau | After registration | Varies |
| Work permits (for foreign staff) | Ministry of Labor and Social Affairs | 2-4 weeks | ETB 5,000+ per person |

### Ongoing Ethiopian Compliance
| Obligation | Frequency | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| CIT return (30%) | Annual | Due within 2 months of fiscal year end |
| Withholding tax on payments to non-residents | Per transaction | 10% on dividends; 10% on services (no DTT with Estonia) |
| Employee income tax withholding | Monthly | Progressive rates up to 35% |
| Pension contribution | Monthly | 7% employee + 11% employer |
| Annual financial statements | Annual | Submit to tax authority and EIC |
| Foreign exchange reporting | Per transaction | National Bank of Ethiopia approval for dividend repatriation |
| Investment permit renewal | Annual | EIC |

**⚠️ Ethiopia-specific caution**: Currency controls are strict. Budget 2-4 weeks for dividend repatriation approvals. National Bank of Ethiopia may require documentation of the underlying investment, tax clearance, and board resolution authorizing the distribution.

## Sanctions Screening Requirements

### Who Must Screen
- **Estonian OÜ (GP)**: EU sanctions (directly applicable) + OFAC (if any USD transactions or US persons involved)
- **Delaware LP**: OFAC sanctions (mandatory for US entities)
- **UAE entities**: UN sanctions + UAE-specific sanctions
- **Ethiopian entities**: Ethiopian sanctions (limited; mostly follow UN)

### What to Screen
1. **LP onboarding**: Screen all prospective LPs against EU, OFAC, and UN sanctions lists
2. **Portfolio company investments**: Screen target companies, their founders, key personnel, and UBOs
3. **Ongoing monitoring**: Re-screen existing relationships at least annually or upon trigger events
4. **Payment counterparties**: Screen all payment recipients before transfers

### Key Sanctions Lists
- EU Consolidated Sanctions List (maintained by European Commission)
- OFAC SDN List (Specially Designated Nationals)
- OFAC Sectoral Sanctions Identifications List
- UN Security Council Consolidated List
- UK Sanctions List (if any UK nexus)

### Tools and Process
- Commercial screening tools: Dow Jones Risk & Compliance, Refinitiv World-Check, ComplyAdvantage
- Document screening results and retain records for 5+ years
- Appoint a sanctions compliance officer (can be the MLRO)
- Establish escalation procedures for potential matches
